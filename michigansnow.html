Snowstorm in Michigan

How Cold is Michigan?

After living in Michigan through two winters, I didn't really think it was as cold as everyone saying it was. During Winter 2015 and 2016, there was not as many snowstorms as I expected.

However, that changed this past winter.

Out Early Morning During a Snowstorm

Early one morning, my father and I had to visit a governmental office in Ypsilanti. Early Friday morning was the only time when both my father and I had time, so we had to go.

When we walked out, much of the snow has not been shoveled yet and snow from the previous snowstorm a couple days ago was still there. The snow was as high as our knees, and there was still more snow falling down.

I have never seen so much snow consistently in a single month. Now I finally believe what everyone meant when they say that it snows a lot in Michigan.
