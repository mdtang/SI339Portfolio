Mother's Birthday

Deciding Where to Eat

It has been too many years since my mother has really celebrated her birthday. We've always had a large dinner at home, but we have never went out to eat at a fine dining restaurant. This was almost always because she was always busy working or wanted to not spend too much money.

I figured that this year is a good year to celebrate her birthday by eating out at Real Seafood Company on Main Street. She deserves it.

What We Ordered

There were so many dishes that we wanted to order, but some of our favorites were:

  • Cream Brulee
  • Seafood Alfredo
  • Miso-Glazed Sea Bass
  • Jambalaya
  • Seafood Chowder
  • Fish and Chips
